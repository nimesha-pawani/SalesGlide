Facilities available


What a Salesman can do using Salesglide?

  • Can add daily sales to the system.

  • Can view the product details which are available.

Other facilities available in Salesglide

  • Can store and maintain the details of the products available.

  • Can get auto-generated sales reports by just providing the required time period.

  • Can manage employees working under sales department.
